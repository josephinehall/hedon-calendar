Step 1: Check availability and rates

Select the date you’d like to confirm availability for. If the date is unavailable, click here to add yourself to the waitlist, or select another date.

If you’d like a single night, click ‘Book a single night’ and select that date in the first calendar display to view the standard rates for that date for up to two people. Click ‘Continue’ to proceed to booking.

If you’d like multiple nights, click ‘Book multiple nights’, select your check-in date on the first calendar, and your check-out date on the second calendar. Standard rates will display. Click ‘Continue’ to proceed to booking.

If you have a discount or voucher code, it can be used in the next step to confirm your modified rate.
